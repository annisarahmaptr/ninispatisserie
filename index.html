About Us

Do you want to know the history of ninisPatisserie?

Ninis Patisserie has a captivating history that traces its origins back to the early 2019. Founded by a visionary pastry chef named Annisa, the patisserie was born out of her unwavering love for baking and a desire to share her creations with the world. Annisa's dedication to her craft was unparalleled, and she meticulously honed her skills to create exquisite pastries that captured the essence of French baking. From the very beginning, Ninis Patisserie was an instant success, attracting patrons with its delightful array of cakes, pastries, and confections.

Today, Ninis Patisserie stands as a testament to the enduring artistry of its founder, captivating taste buds with its delectable treats and carrying forward the rich history of Annisa's vision and expertise.
